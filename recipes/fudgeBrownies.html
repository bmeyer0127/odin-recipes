Fudge Brownies

Truly the best and fudgiest brownies I have ever had

  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate
  • 12 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-pourpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line and 8in square baing dish with parchment paper
  2. Chop chocolate into chunks. Melt half and save other half
  3. In a large bowl, mix butter and sugar with electric mixer
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla for 1-2 minutes. Until it becomes light and fluffy
  5. Whisk in melted chocolate (not too hot)
  6. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Folt in to incorperate
  7. Folt in chocolate chunks and transfer to baking dish
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, depending on how fudgy you like your brownies. Cool completely
